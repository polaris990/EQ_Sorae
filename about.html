About Page

SORAE is a personal creative website developed to showcase my manga, artwork, and digital content in a structured and professional way. The website acts as a portfolio platform where users can explore my comics, view my artwork, and learn more about my creative process. The website includes multiple sections such as Home, Art Commissions, Speed Paint Video, My Art, and Group. Each page is designed with a clear purpose, allowing users to easily navigate and interact with different types of content. For example, users can read manga in a webtoon-style format, watch speed painting videos, and view artwork along with creation dates. In addition, the website supports my personal brand by promoting art commissions, especially for content creators such as YouTubers. It also connects users to my social platforms like TikTok and Telegram through the Group page. Overall, SORAE is designed to be simple, visually appealing, and user-friendly, while also providing a professional platform to present my creative work and services.
